About

Building upon a legacy of excellence, the XIth Edition of the MACE Model United Nations (MACE MUN) Conference marks yet another milestone in the institution's tradition of fostering redefining ideas and nurturing future leaders by emphasizing the ability to APPREHEND global challenges, ACKNOWLEDGE diverse perspectives and solutions, and ACCORD in goodwill to promote collective progress. Since its inception in 2014, MACE MUN has been a distinguished platform for simulating the functioning of the United Nations, allowing delegates to navigate complex global issues. This year, an online committee will function as an additional feature, expanding accessibility and diversity. By incorporating a virtual platform, we are opening the doors to delegates from across the globe, offering a unique opportunity for the participation of a truly international audience. Delegates can tackle complicated international challenges in a dynamic, cooperative setting, and receive unmatched exposure to worldwide diplomacy. Guided by an accomplished Executive Board composed of distinguished judges, the conference shapes its agenda by addressing critical global issues that threaten harmony and stability. Join us at MACE MUN’25 as we honour the spirit of unity, leadership, and negotiation – where ideas transcend borders and the seeds of change are sown.
